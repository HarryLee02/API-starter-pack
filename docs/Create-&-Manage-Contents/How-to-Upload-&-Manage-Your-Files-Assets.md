<h2 id="h_01HPRFWS1FTQSVXTSAYN0FV1SW"><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large"><strong>Introduction:</strong></span></h2>
<p>This article will walk you through how to manage your files/ contents.<br>You can upload, search, preview, download, delete, add to playlists, replace thumbnails, duplicate assets, and replace assets.</p>
<p> </p>
<h2 id="h_01HPRFWS1GD2S2AY397HJTJN3N"><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large"><strong>Upload:</strong></span></h2>
<p class="rich-content-viewer_text__XzvDs rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB">Go to <a href="https://app.optisigns.com/app/assetManagement" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Files/Assets</a> page, and click <strong>Upload Files</strong>.</p>
<p class="rich-content-viewer_text__XzvDs rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804881082387"></p>
<p>You can drag files from your File Explorers or Finders here. Or connect with your Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, etc. and select the files you want to upload to OptiSigns.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804881082899" width="599" height="428"></p>
<p>Click "<strong>Upload</strong>"</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804874311827" width="601" height="445"></p>
<p>You will see messages saying that your files are successfully uploaded, and you will see the files as well.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804881083667"></p>
<h2 id="h_01HPRFWS1GGB74Y6Y628JYGY7J"><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large"><strong>Search:</strong></span></h2>
<p>If you have a large number of files and contents, you can quickly search through your contents to find the one you need. Simply enter some keywords in the search box. The Web Portal will search as you type for any files containing the phase you typing in.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804563157907"></p>
<h2 id="h_01HPRFWS1GDPSYSWXYBCXWNSCD"><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large"><strong>Preview/Download Files</strong></span></h2>
<p>If for some reason you need to preview or download the file. Just hover on the file. Then click on the blue download button. A new window will be opened with your file. You can preview there or save a copy to your local drive.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804600676371"></p>
<h2 id="h_01HPRFWS1G2W1H9GB4FT58JZA8"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large">Delete Files:</span></strong></h2>
<p>You can hover on the file and click the three dots of the file. Then select <strong>Delete</strong>.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804565115795"></p>
<p>When prompted for confirmation, click <strong>Delete</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804589576083"></p>
<h2 id="h_01HPRFWS1GMVWNGT8Y1YKSVF5P"><strong>Add to Playlists</strong></h2>
<p>You can hover on the file and click the three dots of the file. Then select <strong>Add to Playlists</strong><br><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804641594515"></p>
<p>You can select your playlist and click Save</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804674021523" width="521" height="299"></p>
<p>The asset will add to your playlists.</p>
<h2 id="h_01HPRFWS1HV0MXAJPF70B343RX"><strong>Replace thumbnail</strong></h2>
<p>You can hover on the file and click the three dots of the file. Then select <strong>Replace thumbnail</strong></p>
<p><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804678938899"></p>
<p>You can drag files from your File Explorers or Finders here. Or connect with your Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, etc. and select the files you want to make it to thumbnail.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804679420563" width="522" height="380"></p>
<h2 id="h_01HPRFWS1H571JH76VSGAQNH3K"><strong>Duplicate Asset</strong></h2>
<p>You can hover on the file and click the three dots of the file. Then select <strong>Duplicate Asset</strong></p>
<h2 id="h_01HPRFWS1H2Y3MQHK3KCD5WR96"><strong><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804681446291"></strong></h2>

<h2 id="h_01HPRFWS1HYPWVDTFJ0QBWJ6W5"><strong>Replacing a file</strong></h2>
<p>You can hover on the file and click the three dots of the file. Then select <strong>Replace asset</strong></p>
<p><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18804614012051"></p>
<p>You can drag files from your File Explorers or Finders here. Or connect with your Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, etc. and select the files you want to replace it.</p>

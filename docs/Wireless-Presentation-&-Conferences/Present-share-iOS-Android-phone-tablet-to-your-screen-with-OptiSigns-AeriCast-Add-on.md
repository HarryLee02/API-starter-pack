<p>In this article, we will guide you on how to present/ share from iOS/ Android phone/ tablet to your screen.</p>
<p>You can download the AeriCast Presenter app from the app store. </p>
<ul>
<li>iOS App Store: <a href="https://apps.apple.com/us/app/aericast-presenter/id1629924738">https://apps.apple.com/us/app/aericast-presenter/id1629924738</a>
</li>
<li>Android App Store: <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.aericast.presenter" target="_self" rel="undefined">https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.aericast.presenter</a>
</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="h_01HPZN4JPWGF0TR69W74QXR0YE" class="rich-content-viewer_headerTwo__3f-vr rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie blog-post-title-font _3aQMT _2J4pr css-x4x4qs rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB"><strong>Let's jump in and get started:</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">Launch the <strong>AeriCast Present</strong> app. Enter the present code.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14557991027603" width="400" height="866"></p>
<p>Click "<strong>Tap to present</strong>"</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14558021130003" width="400" height="866"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">Click "<strong>Start Broadcast</strong>"</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14558027185811" width="400" height="866"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">You will preset your phone/ tablet on your screen.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14558077259795" width="400" height="866"></p>
<p>When you are done presenting, you can click the red button on the top right of the screen. Click "<strong>Stop</strong>"</p>

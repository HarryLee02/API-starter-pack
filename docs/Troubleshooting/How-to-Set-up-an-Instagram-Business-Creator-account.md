<h3 id="h_01JHN9JTM7WXKHN7XQH6QTBXPE" class="_2s4h"><span style="font-size: 15px;">With Instagram Business/Creator account you can:<br></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Display post from your profiles</li>
<li>You can display posts from any public profiles (i.e. <a href="https://www.instagram.com/arsenal">https://www.instagram.com/arsenal</a>)</li>
<li>You can also follow, monitor up to 30 hashtags</li>
<li>Get follower counts (for <a href="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360047501233" target="_self">Instagram Counter App</a>)</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01JHN9JTM896PT2AX6JZ5ZNN47" class="_2s4h"><strong><span style="font-size: 15px;">To set up an Instagram Business/Creator account:</span></strong></h3>
<p class="_2s4h"><span style="font-size: 15px;">Open Instagram app on you phone, you can convert your personal profile to a business account to access features that can help you grow your business.</span></p>
<div class="_5pfd">
<p class="_3p8">To switch your profile to a business account:</p>
<ol class="_341 _3vz5">
<li>Go to your profile and tap <img class="img" src="https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?revision=641770783093270&amp;name=instagram-sidetrayhamburger-ios&amp;density=1" alt=""> in the upper right corner.</li>
<li>Tap <img class="img" src="https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?revision=641770783093270&amp;name=instagram-newsettings-ios&amp;density=1" alt=""><strong>Settings.</strong>
</li>
<li>Tap <strong>Account</strong>.</li>
<li>Tap <strong>Switch to Professional Account</strong>.</li>
<li>Tap <strong>Business</strong>. or <strong>Creator</strong>
</li>
<li>Follow the steps to connect your business account to a Facebook Page associated with your business. <strong>This step is required</strong> for your Instagram Business/Account to work with 3rd party app like OptiSigns. At this time, only one Facebook Page can be connected to your business account.</li>
<li>Add details, like your business category and contact information.</li>
<li>Tap <strong>Done</strong>.</li>
</ol>
<p class="_3p8">You can now use your Instagram with OptiSigns, when log in, authenticate, please use the Facebook account that you linked in Step 6 above.</p>
<p class="_3p8 wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360080393253" alt="mceclip0.png" width="529" height="330"></p>
<p class="_3p8">With a business account, you'll be able to access business features and Instagram Insights. These tools can help you understand who is engaging with your business on Instagram.</p>
<p class="_3p8">You also have the option to display or hide your business category and contact information on your profile. After setting up, go to your profile and tap <strong>Edit Profile</strong>. Go to <strong>Profile Display</strong> under Public Business Information to choose whether you want to hide or display your category label and contact info. Then, tap <strong>Done</strong>.</p>

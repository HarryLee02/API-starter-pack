<p class="rich-content-viewer_text__XzvDs rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB">Select the Stock App.</p>
<p class="rich-content-viewer_text__XzvDs rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360073357254" alt="mceclip2.png"></p>

<p class="rich-content-viewer_text__XzvDs rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB">Enter the details needed.</p>
<p class="rich-content-viewer_text__XzvDs rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360073357274" alt="mceclip3.png" width="564" height="615"></p>
<p class="rich-content-viewer_text__XzvDs rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB"> </p>
<ul>
<li class="rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie">
<u>Name</u>: Identification of your Currency Wall, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will <u><strong>not</strong></u> be displayed on your screens.</li>
<li class="rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie">
<u>Title:</u>  Displayed Title that will show on screen. It will be above your list of ticker symbols.</li>
<li class="rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie">
<span class="wysiwyg-underline">Base Currency</span>: Select the base currency where you want exchange to.</li>
<li class="rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie">
<span class="wysiwyg-underline">Currencies</span>: Select list of currencies you want to exchange from. </li>
<li>
<span class="wysiwyg-underline">Theme:</span> Light and Dark colored backgrounds</li>
<li>
<span class="wysiwyg-underline">Speed:</span> Select Slow, Medium, Fast or Custom refresh rates</li>
</ul>
<div class="rich-content-viewer_text__XzvDs rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color">Click <strong>Save</strong>.</div>
<div class="rich-content-viewer_text__XzvDs rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color">
<br>After Saving, you can preview the wall. It may take several seconds for the app prepare your wall.<br><br>
</div>

<p class="rich-content-viewer_text__XzvDs rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB">You have created your Currency Exchange Wall.<br>You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab. </p>

<p>Beside the <a href="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360044115513" target="_self">Stock Apps</a>, where you can select a list of your own Stock Tickers, you can use Marked Index app for quicker, more general market prices update. You can add one of these app:</p>
<ul>
<li>World Market Index: Display Index for US, UK, Japan, China, Hong Hong indices</li>
<li>US Market Index: S&amp;P 500, Dow Jones industrial Average and NASDAQ indices</li>
<li>Market Index: select from list of other indices around the world</li>
</ul>
<p>Data is updated every 15 min.</p>

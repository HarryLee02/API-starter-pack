<p>MRSS feeds is a popular way for organization to push out multimedia information. Whether you have a well-know MRSS feeds or one from your local community or company, you can put the feed on big screens TV with OptiSigns.</p>
<p>This article will guide you through how to create and use MRSS app with OptiSigns.</p>
<p>With the OptiSigns MRSS app you can:</p>
<ul>
<li>Connect to MRSS URL (we support images, videos, YouTube and Vimeo URLs) - Images, Videos will be downloaded to devices and cache locally, this will improve performance and make content available even when internet is down.</li>
<li>Choose the timing, duration of your images/videos/YouTube/Vimeo</li>
<li>Choose transition effect between your items</li>
</ul>

<p class="rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie"><u>Name</u>: Name of your MRSS Feed app, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will <u><strong>not</strong></u> be displayed on your screens.</p>

<p class="rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie"><u>Transition Effect</u>: Transition effect between your items.</p>
</li>
<li class="rich-content-viewer_unorderedList__1BJwx rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB AvMd_ _310Mz rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB">
<p class="rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie"><u>Transition Speed:</u> How fast you want your transitions to animate.</p>
</li>
<li class="rich-content-viewer_unorderedList__1BJwx rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB AvMd_ _310Mz rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB">
<p class="rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie"><u>Duration for Image</u>: How long each images should show on your screen, default is 10 seconds.</p>
</li>
<li class="rich-content-viewer_unorderedList__1BJwx rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie _3_7DB AvMd_ _310Mz rich-content-viewer_left__2p1aK _158eo _3_7DB">
<p class="rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie"><u>Max Video Duration:</u> Default is 0, which means the app will play each video till the end. You can set it to some other values to prevent too long videos. For example if you set this value to 60 seconds. If a video is longer than 60s, it will get cut off at 60s to play next item. If a video is less than 60s, it will play the duration of the video. This option would be applied to YouTube and Vimeo URLs as well.</p>
</li>
</ul>

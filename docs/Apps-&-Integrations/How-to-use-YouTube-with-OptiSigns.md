<p>Putting YouTube video on your digital signs screens is easy with OptiSigns.<br>You just need to copy the YouTube Link and paste in the YouTube app in OptiSigns.</p>

<ul>
<li class="rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie">
<strong><u>Name</u></strong>: Give your YouTube video a name. This is for displaying in your asset list only. The app will try to get the video's title and use as name, but you can change it to something else.</li>
<li class="rich-content-viewer_elementSpacing__208Ie">
<strong><u>URL</u></strong>: Paste the link to your YouTube video here. This should be the actual URL, not the Share link:</li>
</ul>
<p><img src="https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/39392515861395" alt="firefox_0SsesYq2vw(1).png"></p>
<ul>
<li>
<u><strong>Caption</strong>:</u> If the YouTube video has closed caption, the app will show it. Note: closed caption must be created by the YouTube video author, auto generated caption may not work. </li>
</ul>

</div>
